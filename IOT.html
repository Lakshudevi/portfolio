Power Generation Using Footsteps in IoT

Innovative energy solution through IoT integration

Project Overview

The process of producing electrical power from various energy sources is called **electricity generation**. This project demonstrates an innovative way of harnessing **kinetic energy** generated by footsteps and converting it into **electrical energy**. The system integrates with IoT technology to monitor and optimize power usage.

How It Works

  1. **Energy Conversion**: Footsteps on pressure-sensitive tiles convert kinetic energy into electrical energy using piezoelectric sensors.
  2. **Energy Storage**: The electrical energy generated is stored in a battery system for later use.
  3. **IoT Integration**: The system is equipped with IoT modules to monitor energy generation and usage in real-time.

Features

  • **Eco-Friendly**: Harnesses renewable kinetic energy from human activity.
  • **Smart Monitoring**: IoT integration provides real-time data on energy generation and usage.
  • **Scalable**: Suitable for crowded places like malls, train stations, and stadiums.

Applications

  • Energy generation in crowded areas such as railway stations and airports.
  • Lighting systems in public places using stored energy.
  • IoT-based monitoring for optimized energy usage in smart cities.

Outcome

This project demonstrates a sustainable and smart approach to energy generation. By combining kinetic energy harvesting with IoT, it opens up possibilities for efficient energy utilization in smart environments.
